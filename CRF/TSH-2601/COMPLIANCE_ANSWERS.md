## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This tender is issued by Lembaga Kemajuan Pertanian Muda (MADA) for the supply, delivery, and application of Soil Amendment Material (Bahan Pemulih Tanah) across 1,850 hectares in four MADA regions in Kedah and Perlis, Malaysia.

**Total Coverage:** 1,850 hectares  
- Wilayah I, Perlis: 350 hectares
- Wilayah II, Jitra: 610 hectares  
- Wilayah III, Pendang: 420 hectares
- Wilayah IV, Kota Sarang Semut: 470 hectares

**Completion Period:** 90 days
