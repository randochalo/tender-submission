## 1. VAS FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

### 1.1 VAS Philosophy

Our Value Added Services framework is designed to:

1. **Accelerate Value Realization** - Deliver quick wins alongside core implementation
2. **De-risk the Investment** - Provide extended support and optimization services
3. **Enable Innovation** - Introduce cutting-edge capabilities beyond baseline requirements
4. **Build Capability** - Transfer knowledge and build MMF's internal competency
5. **Ensure Long-term Success** - Partner for continuous improvement post go-live

### 1.2 VAS Selection Criteria

Each VAS item has been selected based on:

| Criteria | Weight | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| **Business Impact** | 30% | Direct contribution to MMF's operational or financial performance |
| **Strategic Alignment** | 25% | Support for MMF's long-term business objectives |
| **Implementation Readiness** | 20% | Proven capability to deliver with existing technology |
| **Differentiation** | 15% | Unique value not typically offered by competitors |
| **Knowledge Transfer** | 10% | Building MMF's internal capabilities |

### 1.3 VAS vs. Standard Industry Practice

| Aspect | Industry Standard | SESB VAS Offering |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Software Value-Adds** | Basic reports, minor customizations | AI modules, digital twin, blockchain verification |
| **Support Duration** | 3-6 months | 3 years comprehensive |
| **Consultation Hours** | 40-80 hours | Unlimited strategic consulting |
| **Training** | Standard classroom sessions | Multi-modal, unlimited users, certification |
| **Innovation** | Reactive feature requests | Proactive innovation roadmap |
