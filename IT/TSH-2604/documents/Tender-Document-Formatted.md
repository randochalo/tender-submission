## 3. Scope of Work

### 3.1 Tenderer Responsibilities

The Tenderer will be responsible for performing the following tasks:

1. **Supply and Installation**
   - Supply, delivery, installation, testing, and commissioning of software
   - Implementation of IT System for MMF across all branches

2. **System Blueprint**
   - Provide blueprint for each system
   - Provide adequate training in operating the proposed software

3. **Data Migration**
   - Provide resources including labour
   - Assist in migrating MMF's existing data (both softcopy and hardcopy) into IT System

4. **Support Services**
   - Provide support services for software and IT System application
   - 24/7 system availability
   - Dedicated helpdesk service

### 3.2 Training and Documentation

- System Administration Manual
- Database Design documentation

- **Administrator and IT team Training** — Minimum officers as determined by MMF

## 4. System Requirements

### 4.1 Business Operating System (BOS)

#### a) Haulage Management System (HMS) — Annexure C1

**Core Features:**
- Customer management with credit limit and blacklist tracking
- Container management (booking, tracking, returns)
- Job management (create, update, track status)
- Driver and vehicle management
- GPS tracking integration
- Real-time shipment visibility
- Billing and invoice management
- KPI dashboards and reporting

#### b) Forwarding Management System (FFS) — Annexure C2

**Core Features:**
- Ocean freight and air freight management
- Shipment booking and tracking
- Documentation management (B/L, AWB, invoices)
- Customs declaration integration
- Consolidation and deconsolidation (LCL/FCL)
- Multi-currency support
- Automated freight rate calculations
- Customer portal for self-service

#### c) Warehouse Management System (WMS) — Annexure C3

**Core Features:**
- Inventory management (real-time tracking)
- Goods receipt and put-away
- Picking and packing optimization
- Stock transfer between locations
- Cycle counting and stock adjustments
- Barcode/RFID integration
- Space utilization management
- Integration with BOS systems

#### d) Terminal Management System (TMS) — Annexure C4

**Core Features:**
- Container yard management
- Gate-in/gate-out operations
- Container inspection and repair tracking
- Yard planning and optimization
- EDI integration with shipping lines
- Billing for terminal services
- Equipment management (cranes, tractors)
- Reporting and analytics

### 4.2 Support System

#### e) Finance Management System (FMS) — Annexure C5

**Core Features:**
- General ledger and accounting
- Accounts payable and receivable
- Multi-currency transactions
- Automated billing from operational systems
- Cost center and profit center accounting
- Financial reporting and dashboards
- Integration with BOS modules
- Audit trail and compliance
