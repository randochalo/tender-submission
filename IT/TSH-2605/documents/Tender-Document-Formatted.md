## 3. Scope of Work

### 3.1 Tenderer Responsibilities

The Tenderer will be responsible for performing the following tasks:

1. **Supply and Installation**
   - Supply, delivery, installation, testing, and commissioning of software
   - Implementation of IT System for MMF across all branches

2. **System Blueprint**
   - Provide blueprint for each system
   - Provide adequate training in operating the proposed software

3. **Data Migration**
   - Provide resources including labour
   - Assist in migrating MMF's existing data (both softcopy and hardcopy) into IT System

4. **Support Services**
   - Provide support services for software and IT System application
   - 24/7 system availability
   - Dedicated helpdesk service

### 3.2 Training and Documentation

The Tenderer must provide:

**Documentation:**
- Complete User Manual
- Technical Design Manual
- System Administration Manual
- Database Design documentation

**Training Categories:**
- **Administrator and IT team Training** — Minimum officers as determined by MMF
- **User Training** — On-the-job training approach for proper operation

## 5. Terms and Conditions

### 5.1 Warranty

- Helpdesk (after-sales support) service
- Preventive and corrective maintenance
- Onsite and remote support services

### 5.2 Liquidated and Ascertained Damages (LAD)

- **Rate:** 0.5% per working day of Contract Sum for delayed deliverables
- **Accumulation:** Carried forward to next phase
- **Termination:** MMF may terminate contract after unreasonable delay

### 5.3 Extension of Time

Valid causes for extension (with 1 month notice):
- Hostilities, invasion, armed conflict
- Natural disasters (earthquakes, floods, fires)
- Strikes, terrorism, sabotage
- Other force majeure events

### 5.4 Confidentiality

The Successful Tenderer must:
- Maintain strict confidentiality of all information
- Sign Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
- Not disclose information to third parties without written consent
- Take reasonable steps to protect confidentiality
