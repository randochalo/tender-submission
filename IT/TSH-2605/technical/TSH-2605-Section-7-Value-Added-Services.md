## 7.1 ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE & MACHINE LEARNING

### 7.1.1 AI-Powered Predictive Maintenance (EMS)

**Description:**
Advanced machine learning models analyze equipment sensor data, maintenance history, and operational patterns to predict failures before they occur.

**Features:**
- **Failure Prediction:** 60-90 day advance warning with 85%+ accuracy
- **Optimal Maintenance Timing:** Recommend service windows to minimize disruption
- **Parts Demand Forecasting:** AI predicts parts needs to optimize inventory
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis:** Automatic replacement vs. repair recommendations

**Value Proposition:**

| Metric | Before AI | With AI | Improvement |
|--------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Unplanned Downtime | 15% | 4% | **-73%** |
| Maintenance Cost | RM 2.4M/year | RM 1.6M/year | **-33%** |
| Parts Inventory | RM 800K | RM 480K | **-40%** |
| Equipment Lifespan | 8 years | 10+ years | **+25%** |

**Deliverable:**
- AI Analytics Engine deployed on Awan Kita
- Monthly predictive maintenance reports
- Integration with existing maintenance workflows
- 24-month model refinement included
