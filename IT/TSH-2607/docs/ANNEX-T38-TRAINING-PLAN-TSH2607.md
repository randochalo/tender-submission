# ANNEX T38: TRAINING PLAN

## TENDER REFERENCE: TSH-2607
## PROJECT: Universal Service Provision (USP) Claims Management System (UCMS)
## CLIENT: Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC)
## VERSION: 1.0
## DATE: June 2025

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This annex defines the comprehensive training plan for the USP Claims Management System, ensuring all users acquire the necessary knowledge and skills to effectively utilize the system.

### 1.1 Training Standards
- **ADDIE Model**: Analysis, Design, Development, Implementation, Evaluation
- **Bloom's Taxonomy**: Learning objectives framework
- **Kirkpatrick Model**: Training evaluation levels

## 9. EVALUATION FRAMEWORK

### 9.1 Kirkpatrick Evaluation Levels

| Level | Measure | Method | Timing |
|-------|---------|--------|--------|
| 1 - Reaction | Satisfaction | Survey | End of training |
| 2 - Learning | Knowledge gain | Test | Pre/Post training |
| 3 - Behavior | Application | Observation | 30 days post |
| 4 - Results | Business impact | Metrics | 90 days post |

### 9.2 Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Satisfaction Score | > 4.0/5 | Post-training survey |
| Knowledge Test Pass | > 85% | Assessment results |
| Practical Assessment | > 90% | Skills demonstration |
| Completion Rate | > 95% | Attendance records |
| Application Rate | > 80% | 30-day survey |
| Support Ticket Reduction | -50% | Monthly tracking |
