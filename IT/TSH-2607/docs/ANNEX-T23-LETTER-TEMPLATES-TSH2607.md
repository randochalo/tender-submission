# ANNEX T23: LETTER TEMPLATES

## TENDER REFERENCE: TSH-2607
## PROJECT: Universal Service Provision (USP) Claims Management System (UCMS)
## CLIENT: Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC)
## VERSION: 1.0
## DATE: June 2025

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This annex defines the standardized letter templates for all official communications between MCMC and USP Service Providers within the Claims Management System. These templates ensure consistent, professional, and compliant correspondence.

### 1.1 KRISA Compliance
- **KRISA Section 11**: Electronic communications standards
- **National Archives Act 2003**: Record keeping requirements
- **MCMC Corporate Communications Guidelines**: Brand and tone standards
