## SLA COMMITMENTS

### System Availability

| Metric | Commitment | Measurement |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| System Uptime | 99.95% | Monthly calculation |
| Planned Downtime | <4 hours/month | Off-peak hours |
| Emergency Maintenance | 2 hours notice | As required |

### Response Times

| Severity | Definition | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|----------|------------|---------------|-------------------|
| P1 - Critical | Complete system down | 30 minutes | 4 hours |
| P2 - High | Major function impaired | 2 hours | 8 hours |
| P3 - Medium | Minor function impaired | 4 hours | 2 business days |
| P4 - Low | Cosmetic issues | 1 business day | 5 business days |

### Performance Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Page Load Time | <2 seconds |
| API Response Time | <1 second |
| Report Generation | <30 seconds |
| Concurrent Users | 180+ |

### Penalties

| SLA Breach | Penalty |
|------------|---------|
| Uptime < 99.95% | Service credit: 1% per 0.01% below |
| Response time exceeded | Escalation to vendor executive |
