## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 Introduction

As part of our commitment to delivering exceptional value to the Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC), we propose a comprehensive **Value Added Services (VAS) Framework** that extends beyond the core UCMS requirements. This framework comprises **20 distinct value-added services** organized into two categories:

- **Part 1: Software & Innovation Services** (10 services)
- **Part 2: Support & Consultative Services** (10 services)

### 1.2 Value Proposition

| Category | Services | Estimated Value (RM) |
|----------|----------|---------------------|
| Part 1: Software & Innovation | 10 | 300,000 |
| Part 2: Support & Consultative | 10 | 260,000 |
| **Total** | **20** | **560,000** |

**All VAS items are provided at NO ADDITIONAL COST to MCMC.**

### 1.3 Alignment with MCMC Objectives

Our VAS framework directly supports MCMC's strategic objectives:

1. **Digital Transformation** - Advanced analytics and automation
2. **Operational Excellence** - Process optimization and best practices
3. **Stakeholder Satisfaction** - Enhanced user experience and support
4. **Compliance & Governance** - Robust audit and risk management
5. **Future-Readiness** - Scalable and adaptable solutions

## 2. VAS PHILOSOPHY

### 2.1 Why Value Added Services?

In today's rapidly evolving technology landscape, a system's success depends not just on meeting baseline requirements, but on its ability to deliver transformative value. Our VAS framework ensures:

- **Competitive Advantage** - Features that differentiate MCMC's operations
- **Future-Proofing** - Capabilities that adapt to emerging needs
- **Maximum ROI** - Extended value without extended costs
- **Partnership Approach** - Long-term commitment to MCMC's success

### 2.2 VAS Selection Criteria

Each VAS item was selected based on:

1. **Relevance** - Direct applicability to USP operations
2. **Impact** - Measurable improvement to processes
3. **Feasibility** - Technical and practical viability
4. **Innovation** - Cutting-edge capabilities
5. **Sustainability** - Long-term value delivery

   - Table extraction from complex layouts
   - Handwriting recognition enhancement

   - Automatic detection of blurry or incomplete scans
