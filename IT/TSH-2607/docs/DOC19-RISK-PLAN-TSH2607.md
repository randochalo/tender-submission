## RISK REGISTER

| ID | Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation Strategy |
|----|------|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| R01 | USPFS integration complexity | High | Medium | Early POC, dedicated integration team |
| R02 | Data migration issues | High | Medium | Parallel running, validation scripts |
| R03 | User adoption resistance | Medium | Medium | Change management, training |
| R04 | Security vulnerabilities | High | Low | Security by design, VAPT |
| R05 | Resource availability | Medium | Low | Backup resources, cross-training |
| R06 | Scope creep | Medium | High | Strict change control |
| R07 | Performance issues | Medium | Medium | Performance testing, optimization |

## RISK RESPONSE STRATEGIES

- **Avoid:** Eliminate risk through alternative approach
- **Mitigate:** Reduce probability or impact
- **Transfer:** Insurance, third-party
- **Accept:** Monitor and contingency plan
