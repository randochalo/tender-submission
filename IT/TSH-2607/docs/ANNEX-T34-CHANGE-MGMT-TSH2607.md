# ANNEX T34: CHANGE MANAGEMENT PLAN

## TENDER REFERENCE: TSH-2607
## PROJECT: Universal Service Provision (USP) Claims Management System (UCMS)
## CLIENT: Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC)
## VERSION: 1.0
## DATE: June 2025

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This annex defines the Change Management Plan for the USP Claims Management System implementation, ensuring smooth transition from current processes to the new system with minimal disruption to operations and maximum user adoption.

### 1.1 KRISA Compliance
- **KRISA Section 17**: Change management for ICT projects
- **ITIL 4**: Change enablement best practices
- **Prosci ADKAR**: Change management methodology

---

## 2. CHANGE MANAGEMENT STRATEGY

### 2.1 Change Impact Assessment

| Stakeholder Group | Impact Level | Key Concerns | Change Required |
|-------------------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| MCMC Management | High | ROI, Reporting, Compliance | Process oversight |
| Claims Officers | Very High | Daily work changes, Efficiency | Complete workflow change |
| Finance Team | High | Payment processing, Integration | System integration |
| IT Support | High | New system maintenance | Skills development |
| Service Providers | Very High | New submission process, Portal | New interface adoption |
| Auditors | Medium | Audit trail, Reporting | New verification methods |

### 2.2 ADKAR Model Application

| Phase | Objective | Timeline | Activities |
|-------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Awareness | Why change is needed | Weeks 1-4 | Communications, town halls |
| Desire | Motivate to participate | Weeks 5-8 | Benefits, WIIFM, incentives |
| Knowledge | How to change | Weeks 9-16 | Training, documentation |
| Ability | Implement change | Weeks 17-20 | Practice, coaching, support |
| Reinforcement | Sustain change | Ongoing | Recognition, metrics, continuous improvement |

## 7. RISK MITIGATION

### 7.1 Change Resistance Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| User resistance to new system | Medium | High | Early involvement, clear benefits |
| SP adoption challenges | High | High | Extensive training, support hotline |
| Productivity dip at go-live | High | Medium | Parallel run, extra staffing |
| Knowledge gaps post-training | Medium | Medium | Super user support, job aids |
| Process workarounds | Medium | High | Monitoring, reinforcement |

### 7.2 Resistance Management

| Resistance Type | Indicator | Response |
|-----------------|-----------|----------|
| Denial | "This won't affect me" | Personal impact discussion |
| Anger | Active opposition | Listen, acknowledge, involve |
| Bargaining | "I'll do it my way" | Clear expectations, support |
| Depression | Disengagement | Coaching, peer support |
| Acceptance | Engagement | Recognition, advancement |
