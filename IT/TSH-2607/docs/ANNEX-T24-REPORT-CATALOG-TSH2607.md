## 1. PURPOSE

This annex provides a comprehensive catalog of all reports generated by the USP Claims Management System, including operational reports, management reports, regulatory reports, and analytical dashboards.

### 1.1 KRISA Compliance
- **KRISA Section 13**: Transparency and reporting requirements
- **National Audit Department Guidelines**: Financial reporting standards
- ** Treasury Circulars**: Government reporting requirements
